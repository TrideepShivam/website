The Capital of INDIA.

Delhi is the Capital of India. It is the Heart of our Nation. Here lies the Parliament and Supreme Court of the country. It is located on the Yamuna river, and borderd by Haryana and Uttar Pradesh. Traditionally seven cities have been associated with the region of Delhi. The earliest, Indraprastha, is part of a literary description in the Sanskrit epic Mahabharat which situates a city on a knoll on the banks of the river Yamuna. According to art Catherine B. Asher, the topographical description of the Mahabharata matches the are of Purana Qila, a foutreenth-century CE fort of the Delhi sultanate, but the analoggy does not go much further. Whereas the Mahabharata speaks of a beautifully decorated ciry with surrounding fortification, the excavations have yielded "uneven findings of painted gray pottery characteristic of the eleventh century BCE. No signs of a built environment, much less fortification have been revealed."
